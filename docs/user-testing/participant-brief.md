# Participant Brief

## What you are doing

You will use a web application that checks geospatial datasets for quality problems and suggests fixes. We want to see how easy it is to upload data, review issues, choose corrections, and read a report.

## What we need from you

- **Think aloud:** Say what you are trying to do and what surprises you.
- **Honest feedback:** Confusing parts help us improve the tool.
- **Typical behavior:** Use the interface as you would for your own work.

## What we are not testing

- Your GIS expertise or speed
- Memorizing steps from a tutorial

## Logistics

- Session length: about 45–60 minutes
- You may ask clarifying questions about *task goals* (not where buttons are, unless you are stuck for several minutes)
- You can pause or withdraw at any time

## Privacy

Sessions use anonymous IDs (e.g., P01). Personal details are not published in the project repository without separate consent.

## Equipment

- A laptop with Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
- The moderator will provide the path to `sample.geojson` for upload

You will receive tasks one at a time from the moderator.
